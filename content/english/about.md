I am a postdoctoral researcher at CASUS-HZDR (Germany), studying animal-road interactions. My PhD was based in Thailand, working on the spatial-temporal patterns of wildlife-vehicle collisions in a Thai biodiversity hotspot, with a secondary focus on bat communities. I also oversee and conduct statistical analyses for a reptile telemetry project, modeling species movement patterns. I am particularly interested in community ecology, movement ecology, and road ecology.
